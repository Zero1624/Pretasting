Our Story

From Classmates to Business Partners

Pre-Taste Restaurant was born from the friendship and shared dreams of 16 senior high school classmates. What started as simple snack sales during school events grew into a passion for creating innovative Filipino appetizers.

During our time together, we worked on countless food projects, shared meals during breaks, and dreamed of someday opening our own food business. Some of us pursued culinary programs, others studied design and marketing, but we all stayed connected by our love for Filipino food and our commitment to each other.

After graduation, we combined our unique skills—from cooking and plating to branding and customer service—to create Pre-Taste. Today, we proudly serve appetizers made with effort, care, and genuine love for good food, staying true to the dream we built together in high school.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Goals

Our Mission

To continuously provide excellent, freshly made, and distinctively designed appetizers that combine creativity and tradition. By providing exceptional customer service, managing food responsibly, and being dedicated to ongoing development and culinary innovation, our restaurant hopes to provide delightful dining experiences.

Our Vision

To become a well-known and successful local food brand that celebrates innovation in every bite by transforming traditional Filipino snacks into tasty, inventive appetizers that unite people.

Our Goals

  • Offer inventive and distinctive appetizer options that showcase Filipino tastes with a contemporary spin
  • Ensure customer satisfaction by sustaining excellent customer service, cleanliness, and consistent product quality
  • Develop a loyal customer base by providing reasonable costs, unique tastes, and a welcoming environment
  • Promote staff professionalism and food safety by giving employees the proper training and uniform standards
  • Expand the business by adding new, inventive menu items and eventually other locations or catering services

Regional Heritage

Flavors of the Philippines

Philippines

Our menu is a culinary journey across the Philippine archipelago, bringing together the distinct flavors and traditions of different regions. Each dish tells a story of local heritage, passed down through generations and lovingly recreated with a modern twist.

Mindanao - Kinilaw Bicol - Kinunot, Laing Negros Occidental - Lumpiang Ubod Bacolod - Chicken Inasal Cebu - Ginabot, Humba Aklan - Ginataang Bilo-bilo Bohol - Cassava Leyte - Binagol Eastern Samar - Moron, Tambis-Tambis

From the fresh seafood of Mindanao to the coconut-rich dishes of Bicol, every appetizer celebrates the unique culinary identity of its region.
